SPA & WELLNESS

An award-winning spa in Abu Dhabi.

Explore a world of healing at our Abu Dhabi spa hotel, drawing on traditions from across Arabia and Asia. Located on the edge of the city overlooking tranquil mangroves, Anantara Spa offers an escape from city life, with exercise classes, spa relaxation and healthy cuisine.

Discover some of the most extensive hammam rituals in the region. Beautify from head to toe with high-tech yet non-invasive skin and body treatments.
